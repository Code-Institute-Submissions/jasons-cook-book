    • How do I add a recipe or category?
    • How do I edit a recipe or category?
    • I accidentally deleted something...can I get it back?!
    • Can I add more tags?
    You can add recipes and categories by clicking the respective buttons in the top right hand corner of the Cook book, or, if you don't have any recipes or categories saved, then you will be prompted to create a new one when you access the recipe and category menus.
    Once you have created a recipe or category, you can edit them by click the 'edit' button on the the tab for the item you want to edit.
    Unfortunately once you have deleted a recipe, it cannot be recovered.
    As a user, you cannot add more tags than what has been provided, however you can email me at jason.street@learningpeople.co.uk and let me know if you have any suggestions for things to add!
